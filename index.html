Q: what are difference of between Local Storage Vs Session Storage?

Ans.: The minimum requirements are as follows:
Local Storage:-
1. Local Storage data will be availabe after closing the webpage tab.
2. Local Storage data will be removed expire date to reach.
3. Local Storage data reserve for showing next time to reload the webpage.

Session Storage:-
1. SessionStorage will not be available after closing the webpage tab.
2. SessionStorage has no expire date.
3. SessionStorage data does not reserve for next time show.
